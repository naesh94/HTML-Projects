George Washington, the first President of the United States


Here's a time-line of George Washington's life:


  • 1732 - George Washington was born on February 22, 1732, in Westmoreland County, Virginia.

  • 1752 - Washington became a land surveyor at the age of 20 and played a significant role in surveying the western frontier of Virginia.

  • 1775 - George Washington was appointed as the Commander-in-Chief of the Continental Army during the American Revolutionary War.

  • 1789 - He was elected as the first President of the United States and served two terms from 1789 to 1797.

  • 1799 - George Washington passed away on December 14, 1799, at Mount Vernon, Virginia.
